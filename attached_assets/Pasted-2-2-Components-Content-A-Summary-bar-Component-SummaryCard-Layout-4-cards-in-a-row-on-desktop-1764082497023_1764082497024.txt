2.2 Components & Content
A. Summary bar

Component: SummaryCard

Layout: 4 cards in a row on desktop, 2x2 on tablet/mobile.

Each card has:

Title (small label)

Primary metric

Subtext (how it’s calculated / timeframe)

Trend pill (optional)

Cards (example):

“Potential incremental revenue”

Value: +₺1.3M

Subtext: If all high-confidence recs are applied in the next 30 days

Trend: ▲ +18% vs previous 30 days

“High-confidence recommendations”

Value: 12

Subtext: Budget & visibility actions with confidence ≥80%

“Budget at risk”

Value: ₺450K

Subtext: Spend in low-ROAS campaigns flagged by AI

“Applied this period”

Value: 7

Subtext: Recommendations marked as applied

B. Filter bar

Component group: FilterBar

Objective dropdown

Label: Objective

Options: Maximize revenue, Maximize Omni-ROAS, Increase store visits, Clear inventory

Channels multi-select

Label: Channels

Options: All, Google, Meta, TikTok, Other

Region selector

Label: Region

Levels: Country, Region, City, Store cluster

For UI: one dropdown with search.

Category selector

Label: Category

Options from product taxonomy (e.g. Home, Kitchenware, Electronics, Fashion…)

Confidence toggles

Checkbox or chip: High (80–100%), Medium (60–80%)

Default: High checked.

View mode switch

Label: View

Segmented control: ● Cards ○ Table

Apply filters button (optional)

If you want explicit apply vs auto: Apply filters

C. Main content – Recommendation list + Detail panel
Left: Recommendation list

Container: RecommendationList

Shows either Card view or Table view depending on View switch.

Scrolls independently from page.

1) Card view

Component: RecommendationCard

Structure:

Top row

Left: Type badge

e.g. Budget shift, New format, Listing fix, Experiment

Color-coded.

Right: Confidence indicator

e.g. pill with High + progress bar or stars.

Title (one-line, human)

Example:

Shift 15% budget from Meta Prospecting to Google PMax for Kitchenware in Marmara

Impact row

Example values:

Estimated incremental revenue: +₺180,000 / 30 days

Offline ROAS: 3.2x → 3.9x

Affected spend: ₺250,000 / 30 days

Use icons for ROAS, revenue, spend.

Context row

Mini chips:

Channel chips: Google, Meta

Region chip: Marmara

Category chip: Kitchenware

Time-to-impact: Impact in 1–2 weeks

Why we suggest this (short explanation)

2 bullet points, max 1 line each:

Google campaigns in Marmara have 2× higher average store basket value vs Meta for Kitchenware.

Google currently receives only 42% of spend for this combo.

Actions row

Primary buttons (visual only, no backend):

Apply change (primary)

Create experiment

Secondary:

See details (opens detail panel on right)

Overflow icon ⋯ with menu: Snooze, Dismiss

Status indicator (if applied / snoozed)

Small label under actions:

Status: Applied · 3 days ago

Status: Snoozed until 12 Dec

2) Table view
