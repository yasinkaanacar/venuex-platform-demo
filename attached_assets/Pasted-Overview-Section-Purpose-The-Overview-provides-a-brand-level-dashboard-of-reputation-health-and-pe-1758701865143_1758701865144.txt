Overview Section

Purpose: The Overview provides a brand-level dashboard of reputation health and performance. It aggregates data across all locations (unless a location filter is applied) and highlights key KPIs, recent activity, and trends. It’s the default landing for corporate users (e.g. CMO, Head of CX) who need a snapshot of how the brand is doing overall

UX Flow: The Overview is mostly read-only data visualizations. However, everything is interactive to drill down. For instance, clicking on “1★” portion of rating distribution could navigate to Inbox filtered for 1★ reviews.
